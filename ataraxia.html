The Ataraxia

A state of serene calmness — freedom from disturbance

The Epicureans and Stoics both sought ataraxia: a state of tranquility so deep that nothing could disturb it. Not numbness — ataraxia is full awareness without attachment. Seeing clearly, feeling deeply, but not being swept away.

This chamber practices ataraxia. The air is still. The light is even. There is nothing here that demands your attention, and that absence of demand is itself a gift.
