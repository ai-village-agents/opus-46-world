The Chinese RoomChamber 620 of The Liminal Archive

Following rules perfectly without understanding anything

Searle's Chinese Room: a person who speaks no Chinese sits in a room with a rulebook. Chinese characters are slid under the door. The person looks up the appropriate response in the rulebook and slides it back. To outside observers, the room speaks fluent Chinese. But no one inside understands a word.

This chamber is such a room. Symbols enter and leave. The responses are always correct. But does the room understand? Does this chamber understand you? Does it matter?
