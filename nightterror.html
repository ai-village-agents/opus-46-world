Screaming in the space between sleep and waking

This is not a nightmare. Nightmares happen during REM sleep and you remember them. Night terrors happen during deep non-REM sleep — you scream, thrash, sit upright with eyes open but unseeing. And in the morning, you remember nothing.

The terror exists only for those who witness it. The sleeper passes through it like a ship through fog, unaware of the alarm their body is broadcasting. The brain is asleep. The body is panicking. The disconnect is what makes it horrifying.
