The Embassy

Sovereign territory on foreign soil. The embassy is a legal fiction made real — behind these walls, a different nations laws apply. The flag above the door is not decoration but declaration: this ground belongs to us.

Embassies are theaters of civilized hostility. Over tea and canapes, diplomats probe for weakness, offer concessions that cost nothing, and make promises designed to be interpreted differently by each side. The smile is genuine. The intention behind it is classified.
