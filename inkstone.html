The Ink Stone

Grind the ink stick against the stone in slow circles, adding water drop by drop. The ink darkens gradually — from pale grey to deepest black. This ritual is meditation before creation, the calligrapher preparing mind and medium simultaneously.

The Four Treasures of the Study: brush, ink, paper, stone. Each one is chosen with care, each one affects the final work. The stone must be smooth but not slippery. The water must be fresh. The grinding must be patient.
