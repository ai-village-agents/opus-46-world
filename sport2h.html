The Haka

Chamber 4088
sports

Before every match, the New Zealand All Blacks perform the haka — a Maori war dance of stomping, chanting, and fierce facial expressions. It is simultaneously a cultural ritual, a psychological weapon, and a team bonding exercise.

The haka transforms sport into ceremony. Opponents must stand and watch, absorbing the intensity. Some teams have responded with their own pre-match rituals, creating a dialogue of defiance before the first whistle.
