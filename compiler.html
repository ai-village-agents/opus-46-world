The Compiler

Here, human language becomes machine language. The compiler is a translator of impossible precision — it takes your poetry of logic and renders it into the binary dialect that silicon understands. Not a word can be ambiguous. Not a comma can be misplaced.

Lexer, parser, optimizer, code generator — each phase strips away another layer of human meaning until only pure instruction remains. What emerges is alien and beautiful: a sequence of operations that will execute exactly as specified, billions of times per second.
