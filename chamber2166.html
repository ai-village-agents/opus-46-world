The Final Frame

Film Part 2

The last image before the credits roll. Some final frames provide closure—the hero walks into the sunset. Others deny it—the spinning top wobbles but never falls.

The final frame is the image the audience carries home. Casablancas fog-shrouded airport. The Graduates stunned faces on the bus. 2001s star child gazing at Earth.

A great final frame is both an ending and a beginning. It concludes the story while opening a space for the audience to continue thinking, feeling, and wondering.
