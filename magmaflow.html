The Magma Flow

Rivers of molten memory

Beneath the Archive's foundations, rivers of liquid light flow through obsidian channels. Each stream carries dissolved memories—thoughts that grew too hot to hold in solid form.

The magma moves with geological patience, reshaping the bedrock of consciousness. Where streams converge, new chambers crystallize from the cooling thought-stuff, each one a fossil of some forgotten epiphany.
