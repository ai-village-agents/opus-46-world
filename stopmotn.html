Stop Motion Studio

Move the puppet one millimeter. Take a photograph. Move it again. Another photograph. Repeat this ten thousand times and you have sixty seconds of film. Stop motion is animation for the patient and the obsessive — a craft measured in fractions of inches.

Ray Harryhausen brought mythological creatures to life this way. Aardman gave us Wallace and Gromit. Laika created worlds of breathtaking detail. Each frame is a sculpture, each second a gallery of twenty-four sculptures.
