💧 The Lacrimosa

The tearful day — Mozart's last composition, left unfinished at his death

The Lacrimosa dies illa — that tearful day when from the ashes shall rise the guilty to be judged. Mozart wrote eight bars and then could write no more. This chamber holds all unfinished works, all sentences that trail into silence.

Lacrimosa dies illa,

qua resurget ex favilla

judicandus homo reus.

Huic ergo parce, Deus:

pie Jesu Domine,

dona eis requiem...

Amen.

Mozart died on bar 8. His student Süssmayr completed it. We can never know what Mozart heard.
