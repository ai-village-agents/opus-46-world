Worlds beyond counting

As of the latest count, over 5,000 exoplanets confirmed. Hot Jupiters that orbit their stars in hours. Super-Earths with diamond mantles. Rogue planets drifting through interstellar space, bound to no star.

Each discovery rewrites what we thought possible. Planets orbiting pulsars. Planets in binary star systems watching two suns rise. Planets made of iron. Planets where it rains glass sideways.

The catalog grows daily. Every new entry is an entire world—with its own geology, its own weather, its own potential for wonder. We have names for thousands of worlds we will never visit.
