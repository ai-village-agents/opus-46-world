Mnemonics

Memory Techniques

Every Good Boy Does Fine. Roy G. Biv. My Very Educated Mother Just Served Us Nachos. Mnemonics compress complex sequences into memorable phrases.

The best mnemonics are personal, vivid, and slightly absurd. Medical students create elaborate stories to remember cranial nerves. Chemistry students sing the periodic table.

Mnemonics work because they leverage existing knowledge (language, narrative, humor) to carry new information. They are bridges between what you know and what you need to learn.
