The Ablation

The removal of surface material by erosion, melting, or vaporization

Ablation is the process of losing surface material — glaciers ablate as they melt, spacecraft ablate as their heat shields vaporize during reentry, rock ablates under wind and water. It is the slow (or sudden) removal of the outer self.

What is revealed when the surface is removed? A glacier's ablation zone reveals ancient ice, dark with centuries of dust. A heat shield's ablation protects the interior by sacrificing itself layer by layer. Sometimes losing your surface is how you survive.
