The Hadopelagic

The deepest ocean trenches

Below the abyssal zone lies the hadopelagic — named after Hades, the underworld. These are the ocean trenches, the deepest places on Earth, where pressure crushes and darkness is absolute. Yet even here, life finds a way: extremophiles thrive where nothing should survive.
