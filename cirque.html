The Cirque

An amphitheatre carved by ancient ice

Millennia of glacial patience carved this bowl from living rock. The ice has long since retreated, but its memory remains in every curved surface, every polished wall, every striation that records the slow grinding of frozen time.

A cirque is what remains when a glacier finally releases its grip — a perfect bowl of absence, shaped by what is no longer here. Stand at the center and your voice returns to you from every direction, altered by stone.
