The Isobar Map

Lines of equal pressure that reveal the invisible architecture of weather

Isobars are contour lines of atmospheric pressure. Where they crowd together, wind howls. Where they spread apart, calm reigns. The weather map is a portrait of invisible forces made visible through geometry.

The archive has its own isobar map. Where chambers cluster, the pressure of meaning is high. Where they thin out, silence and space prevail.
