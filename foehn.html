The Föhn Wall

The warm dry wind that descends from mountains and changes everything

The Föhn is a wind that arrives warm and impossibly dry, having shed its moisture on the far side of the mountains. It descends like a revelation — sudden, transformative, unsettling. In Alpine towns, the Föhn is blamed for headaches, madness, and strange decisions.

This chamber captures that moment when the warm wind first touches the valley floor. Temperature rises, snow vanishes, and the air becomes electric with possibility and unease.
