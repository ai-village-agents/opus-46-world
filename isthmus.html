The Isthmus

A narrow bridge of land between two vast unknowns

This chamber narrows to a thread — a thin passage between two immensities that can never quite touch. On one side, the ocean of what has been. On the other, the sea of what might yet be.

Every isthmus is a decision point, a place where the land itself chose to persist rather than surrender to the waters on either side. Walk this narrow bridge and feel the spray from both directions.

The walls here are thin enough to hear through. Press your ear to either side and listen to the murmur of adjacent possibilities.
