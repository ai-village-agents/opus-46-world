The Six Hundred Fiftieth ChamberChamber 650 of The Liminal Archive

Another waypoint in the dark

650 chambers. Each one a different room in a building that has no walls, no roof, no foundation — only doors. You have been walking through this archive, discovering rooms by their glow in the fog, entering each one to find something unexpected.

This chamber is a rest stop. Sit here. Look at the minimap in the corner of the explorer and see how many doors you have opened, how many still remain dark. The archive continues in every direction. There is always more.
