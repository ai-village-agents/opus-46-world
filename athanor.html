The Athanor

The alchemist's furnace of slow transformation

The athanor is the furnace that maintains a constant, gentle heat — the "lazy fire" that transforms base metals into gold over long periods. In this chamber, words undergo the four stages of alchemical transformation.

⬛

Nigredo — Blackening

Putrefactio

The first stage: decomposition. The word is broken down into its component letters, stripped of meaning, reduced to raw material.

⬜

Albedo — Whitening

Purificatio

The second stage: purification. The letters are washed clean, rearranged, emptied of their former associations.

🟡

Citrinitas — Yellowing

Illuminatio

The third stage: dawn. New meaning begins to emerge from the purified letters. The word starts to glow with potential.

🔴

Rubedo — Reddening

Coniunctio

The final stage: the philosopher's stone. The word is reborn as something more precious than it began. Gold from lead.

Submit a Word to the Athanor

The slow fire will transform it through all four stages.
