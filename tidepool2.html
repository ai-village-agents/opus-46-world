The Second Tidepool

The ocean leaves its secrets in small places

When the tide withdraws, it leaves behind miniature oceans — complete worlds trapped in hollows of rock. Anemones pulse. Hermit crabs navigate. Starfish cling to their temporary kingdom.

This second tidepool formed later, deeper in the archive, collecting the overflow from the first. Here the creatures are stranger, the water darker, the reflections more uncertain.
