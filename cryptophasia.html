The Cryptophasia

A secret language known only to twins

Cryptophasia: the private language that sometimes develops between twins — a code that no one else can crack because it was never meant for anyone else. It is communication in its purest form: two minds creating meaning between themselves.

This chamber speaks in cryptophasia. The inscriptions on the walls are in a language that exists only here. You cannot learn it. You can only feel its meaning pressing against the edges of understanding.
