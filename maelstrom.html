The Maelstrom

A vortex in the ocean where water spirals into itself

The maelstrom is where the ocean turns inward, creating a spinning vortex that draws everything toward its center. In Norse mythology, it was the grinding mill of the sea gods. In reality, it is a tidal whirlpool, beautiful and terrifying.

This chamber spins slowly around a central point of absolute stillness. The paradox of the vortex: at its most violent center, there is calm. The faster the water moves, the more still its heart becomes.
