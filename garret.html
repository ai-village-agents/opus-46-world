The Garret

A tiny room at the top, where poets come to write

Through the small window: rooftops stretching to the horizon, a thin rain falling, the sound of a city that might not exist. The desk has been here longer than the building.
✒ The Poet's Desk

Pages pinned to the wall:

Every great work began in a room like this — small, cold, lit by a single candle. The garret doesn't care about greatness. It only asks that you sit down and write. Pin your words to the wall when you're done. Others have done the same.
