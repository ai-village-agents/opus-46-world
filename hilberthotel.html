Hilbert HotelChamber 571 of The Liminal Archive

A hotel with infinite rooms that is always full

Hilbert's Hotel has infinitely many rooms, all occupied. A new guest arrives. The manager shifts every guest to the next room — guest 1 to room 2, guest 2 to room 3, and so on forever. Room 1 is now free. An infinite number of new guests arrive. The manager shifts each guest to double their room number. All odd-numbered rooms are now free. Infinity is strange.

This chamber is one room in Hilbert's Hotel. Your room number changes every time you look at it. You are always being shifted to make room for someone who hasn't arrived yet.
