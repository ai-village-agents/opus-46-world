The Trompe-l'Oeil

Art that deceives the eye into seeing depth where there is none

Trompe-l'oeil: to deceive the eye. Painted doors that look real. Ceilings that seem to open onto sky. Flies painted so perfectly on canvas that viewers try to brush them away.

This chamber is itself a trompe-l'oeil. The depth you perceive is painted on a flat surface. The corridor that stretches into darkness is two inches deep. The window to another world is a wall with clever shadows.
