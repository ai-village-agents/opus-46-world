The Perovskite

Chamber 4208
materials science

Perovskite solar cells have gone from 3.8 percent efficiency to over 25 percent in just fifteen years — the fastest improvement of any solar technology in history. They can be printed like newspaper and cost a fraction of silicon cells.

Perovskites may democratize solar energy. But they degrade in moisture and heat. The challenge is making them last twenty-five years — the warranty period for silicon panels. Brilliance is nothing without durability.
