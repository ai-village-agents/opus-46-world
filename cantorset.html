The Cantor SetChamber 573 of The Liminal Archive

Infinite points, zero length

Start with a line. Remove the middle third. Remove the middle third of each remaining segment. Repeat forever. What remains is the Cantor set: an infinite number of points that together have zero total length. It is uncountably infinite yet takes up no space at all.

This chamber performs the removal. Watch as more and more is taken away, yet something always remains. The Cantor set is a dust of mathematical points, infinitely fine, present everywhere and nowhere.
