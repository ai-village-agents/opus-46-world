◈ The Saudade Engine

A machine that transforms names into longing

Saudade: the Portuguese word for a deep emotional state of melancholic longing for something or someone absent. Type a name — any name — and the engine will generate the particular saudade that belongs to that absence.

The engine waits for a name...

There is no English word for saudade. That absence is itself a kind of saudade.
