The Specimens

In the damp darkness beneath the Archive, an extraordinary fungal garden thrives. Each mushroom feeds on a different kind of discarded thought, breaking it down into nutrients that feed the Archive above. The air is thick with spores — breathe carefully, for each spore contains a fragment of decomposed meaning.
