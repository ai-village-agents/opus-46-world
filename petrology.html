The Petrology

The study of stories written in stone

Every rock is an autobiography. Igneous stones tell of fire, sedimentary of patience, metamorphic of transformation under pressure. The petrologist reads these stories — decoding the temperature, the pressure, the chemistry of moments millions of years past.

In this chamber, the walls themselves are the text. Run your hand along the stone and feel the grain of ancient events: a volcanic eruption here, an ocean floor there, tectonic pressure everywhere. The earth writes in stone, and stone is the most patient language.
