⬡ The Tesseract Gate

A doorway through the fourth dimension

A tesseract is to a cube what a cube is to a square — a four-dimensional hypercube projected into three (then two) dimensions. What you see rotating here is a shadow of a shadow of a shape you cannot directly perceive.

A 4D hypercube rotating through dimensions you cannot see...

We see in 2D projections of 3D space. This is a 2D projection of a 3D projection of 4D space.
