The Dyson Dilemma

Chamber 4462
astronomy

Freeman Dyson proposed that advanced civilizations would build structures to capture all energy from their star. A Dyson sphere would be detectable as a star that emits only infrared — all visible light converted to waste heat.

We've searched for Dyson spheres among millions of stars. None found. Either advanced civilizations are rare, or they don't build them, or our detection methods miss them.

Perhaps the absence of Dyson spheres tells us something about the nature of intelligence: that civilizations advanced enough to build them are advanced enough to not need them.
