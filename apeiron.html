∞ The Apeiron

Anaximander's boundless — the indefinite source from which all things emerge

Before there was fire or water, earth or air, there was the apeiron — the unlimited, undefined, from which all opposites separate and to which all things return. Watch forms emerge from formlessness and dissolve back into the boundless.

Anaximander said: "Things perish into those from which they have their birth"
