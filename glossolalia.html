👅 The Glossolalia

Speaking in tongues — language beyond language

Glossolalia is the phenomenon of speaking in a language unknown to the speaker — fluid, phonetically complex, yet carrying no decodable meaning. It exists at the boundary where speech becomes pure sound, where communication becomes communion.

Linguists found glossolalia uses the speaker's native phoneme inventory — even ecstasy speaks with an accent
