Diapause

The art of suspending life until conditions improve

When the world becomes hostile, some organisms enter diapause — a state of suspended animation deeper than sleep, deeper than hibernation. Metabolism drops to nearly zero. Time stops for the creature while the world keeps turning.

Certain chambers in the archive enter diapause during quiet periods. They do not dream. They simply wait.

All life here is suspended. Unless you wake it.
