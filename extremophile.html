Life in impossible places

Extremophiles: organisms that thrive where life should be impossible. Thermophiles in boiling hot springs. Psychrophiles in Antarctic ice. Acidophiles in pH 0 pools. Radioresistant bacteria that survive doses of radiation thousands of times lethal to humans. Life finds a way — every way.

This gallery showcases the most extreme survivors. Each display represents a different impossible environment, and each contains something alive, thriving, reproducing, evolving.
