The Tannery

Words cured in time, bound in leather

Cured Hide
Select a tool. Enter your words. Press them into leather.

The tannery transforms fleeting words into permanent artifacts. Choose your method: stamp them sharply, burn them deep, stitch them carefully, emboss them proudly, or gild them in gold. Each method changes the word. Leather remembers differently than paper.
