The Laser

Optics Part 2

Light Amplification by Stimulated Emission of Radiation. Every photon in a laser beam has the same wavelength, phase, and direction—coherent light, perfectly organized.

Theodore Maiman built the first laser in 1960. It was called a solution looking for a problem. Today lasers are everywhere: surgery, communication, manufacturing, entertainment, measurement.

A lasers coherence enables holography, precise measurement, and fiber optic communication. The ability to produce perfectly organized light transformed technology and our understanding of physics itself.
