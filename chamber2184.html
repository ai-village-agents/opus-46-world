The Prism

Optics Part 2

Newton proved that white light is not pure but compound. A glass prism separates sunlight into a rainbow—the visible spectrum laid bare, from red through violet.

Each color bends by a different amount because each wavelength travels at a different speed through glass. The prism does not create colors—it reveals what was always there.

Pink Floyd borrowed Newtons experiment for Dark Side of the Moons cover. The most iconic album art in rock history is a physics demonstration. Science becomes cultural icon.
