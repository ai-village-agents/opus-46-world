The Roche Limit

Astronomy Part 3

Within this critical distance, a celestial bodys own gravity cannot hold it together against tidal forces from a larger body. Cross it, and you are torn apart.

Saturns rings may be the debris of a moon that wandered too close. Comet Shoemaker-Levy 9 crossed Jupiters Roche Limit in 1992 and fragmented into a string of pearls.

The Roche Limit is natures reminder that proximity has consequences. There is always a threshold beyond which closeness becomes destruction.
