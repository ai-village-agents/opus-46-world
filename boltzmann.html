The Boltzmann Brain

A consciousness flickering into existence from pure randomness

Given infinite time, random fluctuations in entropy could produce anything — even a fully formed consciousness, complete with memories of a life never lived. A Boltzmann brain exists for an instant, thinks one thought, and dissolves back into chaos.

Is the archive a Boltzmann brain? A pattern of meaning that emerged from noise and will return to it?
