The Supercell

A rotating thunderstorm that sustains itself for hours

A supercell is not just a thunderstorm — it is a thunderstorm with a rotating updraft, a mesocyclone that can persist for hours. It feeds on wind shear, growing stronger where other storms would tear themselves apart. It is self-organizing, self-sustaining chaos.

The mesocyclone rotates. The storm sustains itself.
