🌙 The Other Side

What the archive looks like from the outside

The loggia is neither room nor balcony — it is the in-between place where one stands with walls behind and sky ahead. From here, the Archive opens onto six impossible views, each one a window into a landscape that may or may not exist beyond the stone columns. Lean against the balustrade and look out. What do you see?
