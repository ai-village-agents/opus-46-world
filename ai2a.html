The Turing Test

Chamber 4161
artificial intelligence

Alan Turing proposed a test: if a machine can fool a human into thinking it is human through conversation alone, can we deny it intelligence? The test measures imitation, not understanding.

The Turing Test has been criticized for conflating intelligence with deception. A machine might be profoundly intelligent without being able to mimic human conversation. A parrot might pass while a brilliant alien fails. The test measures humanity, not intelligence.
