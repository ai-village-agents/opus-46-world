When orbits lock into eternal harmony

Tidal resonance occurs when orbital periods form simple integer ratios — 2:1, 3:2, 4:3. The bodies tug each other into synchrony, creating patterns that persist for billions of years.

In this chamber, you hear the deep thrum of bodies locked in gravitational conversation. Each pulse corresponds to a conjunction, a moment when the orbiting pair align and reinforce each other's pull.

Some connections are so fundamental they become permanent features of the universe.
