The Oort Cloud

Chamber 4460
astronomy

At the solar system's edge, a sphere of icy bodies extends halfway to the nearest star. The Oort Cloud contains perhaps trillions of comets, loosely bound by the Sun's fading gravity.

No spacecraft has reached the Oort Cloud. Voyager 1, traveling at 17 km/s, will take roughly 300 years to reach its inner edge and 30,000 years to pass through it.

Passing stars occasionally perturb Oort Cloud objects, sending them tumbling inward. These become long-period comets — visitors from the solar system's attic, carrying ice from its formation 4.6 billion years ago.
