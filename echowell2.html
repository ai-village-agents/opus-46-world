The Echo Well

Reflections of reflections

A deep well that doesn't reflect your face but your thoughts. Look down and see not water but a swirling mirror of consciousness—ideas you haven't yet had rising to the surface.

Some visitors report seeing their future selves looking back up at them. Others see nothing at all, which the Archive considers the most profound reflection of all.
