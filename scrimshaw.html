The Scrimshaw

Stories etched into bone, lasting longer than the teller

Sailors once carved their longing into whale teeth — images of home, of lovers left behind, of storms survived. This chamber holds a single great bone, curved like the hull of a ship, and upon it are etched the stories of every visitor who has taken up the needle. The bone remembers. It always remembers.
