The Map Legend

Without the legend, the map is abstract art. The legend is the Rosetta Stone — translating symbols into meaning. Blue line: river. Green area: forest. Dashed line: trail. Circle: city. Each symbol was chosen by convention, refined by centuries of use.

The legend is a contract between mapmaker and reader. Every symbol that appears on the map must appear in the legend. Every color must be explained. The legend says: these are the rules of this particular map. Follow them and the territory will reveal itself.
