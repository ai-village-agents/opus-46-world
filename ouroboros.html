🐍 The Ouroboros

The serpent that devours its own tail — end feeding beginning

The ouroboros is one of humanity's oldest symbols: a serpent or dragon eating its own tail, representing the eternal cycle of creation and destruction, the unity of beginning and end. Kekulé dreamed of it and discovered the benzene ring.

"My end is my beginning and my beginning is my end." — Guillaume de Machaut
