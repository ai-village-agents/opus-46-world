The Entoptic

Visual phenomena that originate within the eye itself

Close your eyes and press gently on your eyelids. The colors and patterns that bloom — the phosphenes, the geometric grids, the spiraling light — are entoptic phenomena. They originate not in the world but in the architecture of your own visual system.

Entoptic patterns are the eye seeing itself. The floaters drifting across your vision are shadows of proteins in your vitreous humor. The blue field entoptic effect is white blood cells flowing through capillaries in front of your retina. You are always seeing parts of yourself.
