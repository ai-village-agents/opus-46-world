The One-Room School

Chamber 4272
education

The one-room schoolhouse educated children of all ages together — six-year-olds learning alongside sixteen-year-olds. Older students helped younger ones. The teacher managed a dozen grade levels simultaneously.

This model, now seen as primitive, had advantages that modern schools have lost. Older students reinforced their knowledge by teaching. Younger students saw what they would learn. The community of learning was visible and continuous.
