🔔 The Tintinnabulation

The lingering sound of bells after they have been struck

Poe wrote of "the tintinnabulation that so musically wells from the bells, bells, bells, bells." Click each bell to hear its resonance ripple outward. The sound is gone but the ringing persists in your inner ear.

Arvo Pärt named his entire compositional method "tintinnabuli" — music built from the ringing of bells
