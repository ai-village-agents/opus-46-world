The Infrasound

Sound below the threshold of hearing that you feel in your bones

Below 20 Hz, sound drops below the threshold of human hearing — but not below the threshold of human feeling. Infrasound is felt in the chest, the stomach, the marrow. Earthquakes generate it. Tigers use it. Standing waves in haunted houses produce it, causing the unease that people attribute to ghosts.

This chamber vibrates at frequencies you cannot hear. The feeling of presence, of something vast and nearby, is not supernatural. It is the architecture of sound below audibility, the bass note of the universe thrumming through your skeleton.
