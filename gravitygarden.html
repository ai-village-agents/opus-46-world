The Gravity Garden

Where weight becomes beauty

In this inverted garden, plants grow downward from a ceiling of dark stone. Their roots reach toward the sky; their flowers bloom facing the ground. Gravity here is a creative force, not a constraining one.

Water flows upward through crystalline stems. Seeds fall toward the ceiling. The garden teaches that any force, understood deeply enough, becomes a medium for art.
