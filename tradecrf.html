Tradecraft

The art of not being noticed. Tradecraft is the professional discipline of espionage — surveillance detection routes, brush passes, dead drops, cover stories, signal sites. Each technique is simple. The mastery is in combining them flawlessly under pressure.

Walk past the window. Do not look. Cross the street at the next corner. Enter the department store. Exit through the side door. Take the metro two stops. Get off. Get back on. If the same face appears three times, you are being followed.
