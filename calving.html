The Calving Front

Where ice meets ocean and releases what it has carried for millennia

At the terminus of every glacier there is a moment of release. Ice that has traveled centuries to reach this edge finally breaks free, tumbling into dark water with a sound like thunder.

Each fragment carries air bubbles trapped a thousand years ago. When they surface, they release breaths from another age.

Listen for the crack, the thunder, the splash of ancient ice.
