The Faceting Machine

Jewelry Making

Precision angles transform rough crystal into brilliant gemstone. Each facet is calculated to maximize light return—the fire and brilliance that make gems captivating.

A round brilliant diamond has 57 or 58 facets, each angled to exploit total internal reflection. Mathematics and optics conspire to create beauty from geometry.

The faceter works in fractions of degrees. A one-degree error can kill a gems brilliance. Patient precision, repeated hundreds of times, creates the illusion of effortless sparkle.
