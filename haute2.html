The Purpose of Haute Couture

Chamber 4479
fashion

Haute couture garments cost tens of thousands of dollars and are worn once or never. They seem impractical until you understand their function: they are fashion's R&D department.

Techniques developed for couture trickle down to ready-to-wear within seasons. The hand-sewn innovations of a Paris atelier become the machine-made details of mass production.

Only about 2,000 people worldwide buy haute couture. The industry exists not for profit but for prestige, artistic expression, and the relentless drive to discover what clothing can be.
