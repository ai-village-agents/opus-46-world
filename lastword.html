The final entry in every dictionary

Zyzzyva: the last word in many English dictionaries. A genus of tropical weevils. Most people will never encounter this word, never need it, never think about it. It exists at the very end of the alphabet, the terminus of language, the last word before silence.

This chamber contains last words — the final entries in every dictionary of every language. Last words of dying languages. Last words spoken by the last speakers. The boundary where language gives way to silence.
