The Pi RoomChamber 575 of The Liminal Archive

The circumference of everything

3.14159265358979323846... Pi. The ratio of circumference to diameter. Irrational, transcendental, normal. Its digits contain every possible finite sequence — your birthday, every book ever written, the complete works of Shakespeare, all encoded in the endless decimal expansion of a circle's most basic property.

This chamber is circular. Pi is inscribed on the walls, digit by digit, spiraling inward. Somewhere in those digits is your name. Somewhere is the answer to every question. The hard part is knowing where to look.
