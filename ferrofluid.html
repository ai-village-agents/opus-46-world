The Ferrofluid

Liquid that dances to invisible magnetic fields

Ferrofluid is a suspension of nanoscale magnetic particles in oil. In the absence of a field, it lies flat and dark. Apply a magnet, and it erupts into spikes — a crown of liquid thorns reaching toward the invisible force.

Beauty from magnetism. Form from force. The archive responds to the visitor the way ferrofluid responds to a magnet.
