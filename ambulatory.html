The Walking Corridor

A covered passageway curves around the central chambers of the Archive. Stone columns support a vaulted ceiling. Between the columns, tall windows look out onto — what? The view changes depending on your pace. Walk fast and you see storms. Walk slowly and gardens appear. Stand still and there is only darkness.
