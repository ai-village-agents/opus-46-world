The Convection Cell

Hot rises, cold sinks, and the world turns

In every pot of boiling water, in every star, in the mantle of the Earth itself, convection cells circulate. Hot material rises, cools, sinks, heats again. This simple cycle drives weather, plate tectonics, and the fusion that lights the sun.

Hot rises, cold sinks. The eternal cycle.
