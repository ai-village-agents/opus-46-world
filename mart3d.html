Muay Thai: The Art of Eight Limbs

Chamber 3861
martial arts

Muay Thai uses fists, elbows, knees, and shins — eight striking surfaces where boxing uses only two.

The clinch in Muay Thai is not a rest position but a fighting position — devastating knee strikes at close range.

Thai fighters begin training as children, with professional careers often starting at age 12.

Muay Thai reminds us that mastery requires starting young, training hard, and using every tool available.
