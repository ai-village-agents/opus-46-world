Deception as survival strategy

Batesian mimicry: harmless species that evolve to look like dangerous ones. Mullerian mimicry: dangerous species that converge on the same warning pattern. Aggressive mimicry: predators that disguise themselves as prey. Evolution is full of liars, and the lies work.

In this gallery, nothing is what it appears. The flower is a mantis. The stick is a caterpillar. The harmless butterfly wears the colors of a toxic species. Every surface is a deception refined over millions of years.
