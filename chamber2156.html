Tango

Dance Part 2

Born in the tenements of Buenos Aires where immigrants from Italy, Spain, and Africa created a new dance from shared displacement. Tango is nostalgia made physical.

The embrace is everything. Chest-to-chest, the leader communicates intention through the subtlest shifts of weight and torso. The follower interprets and responds. Words are unnecessary.

Tango walks are deceptively simple. A single step, taken with full commitment and connection, contains more expression than a dozen flashy figures. The essence lives in the walk.
