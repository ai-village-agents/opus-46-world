The Stromatolite

The oldest visible life on Earth, built layer by layer

Stromatolites are layered mounds built by cyanobacteria over billions of years. The oldest known fossils are stromatolites — 3.5 billion years old. Layer upon layer of microbial mats, trapped sediment, and mineral precipitation. Life's first architecture.

3.5 billion years of patient accumulation.
