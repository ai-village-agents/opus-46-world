The Penumbra

The gradient between knowing and unknowing

In astronomy, the penumbra is the partial shadow between total darkness (umbra) and full light (antumbra). It is the zone of ambiguity — where things are neither fully visible nor fully hidden. This is where most of reality lives.

Umbra

Total shadow. What you cannot see. What you cannot know.

Penumbra

Partial shadow. What you glimpse. What you almost understand.

Antumbra

Beyond the shadow. What blazes clear. What you know you know.
