The PenitentesChamber 583 of The Liminal Archive

Ice needles that pray toward the sun

Penitentes: tall, thin blades of snow or ice that form at high altitude in dry conditions. They point toward the sun, growing up to several meters tall, creating fields of sharp white spires. Named for their resemblance to a procession of monks in pointed hoods.

This chamber is a forest of penitentes. They stand in rows, all angled the same direction, all reaching toward a light source that has long since set. A congregation of ice, forever praying to an absent sun.
