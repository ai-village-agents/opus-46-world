The Living Palimpsest

A text that rewrites itself as you read it

Most palimpsests are dead, their layers fixed in time. But this one lives, its text still changing, still being overwritten and recovered. Old meanings surface as new ones are inscribed. The act of reading changes what is written.

You are reading this text. It is changing because you are reading it. Look away and look back. It will be different.

The text rewrites itself as you watch.
