The Thaumaturgy

The working of wonders; the performance of miracles

Thaumaturgy: from the Greek thauma (wonder) and ergon (work). Not magic but the disciplined practice of producing wonder. The thaumaturge knows that miracles are deeper applications of natural law.

This chamber is a workshop for wonders. The tools are patience, attention, and willingness to be astonished. The raw material is the ordinary world.
