The Brinicle

An icicle of death descending through the ocean

When sea ice forms, it expels brine — water so cold and salty it sinks through the ocean like a stalactite growing downward. This brinicle, this finger of ice, freezes everything it touches on the seafloor. It is winter reaching down through water.

The brinicle is one of nature's most eerie phenomena: a tube of ice growing downward through liquid water, killing sea stars and urchins as it creeps across the bottom. It is entropy made visible, cold made architectural.
