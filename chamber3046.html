The fifth taste, unnamed in the West until 1908 when Kikunae Ikeda isolated glutamate from kelp. Umami is the taste of depth, of satisfaction, of more.

Parmesan, soy sauce, mushrooms, tomatoes — all umami-rich. This taste is why certain combinations feel inevitable. It is the flavor of completeness.
