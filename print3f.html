The Printing Error Museum

Chamber 3713
printing

The Wicked Bible of 1631 accidentally commanded: Thou shalt commit adultery. The printers were fined 300 pounds.

Printing errors — errata, misregistrations, ghosted pages — are the fingerprints of human fallibility in mechanical reproduction.

Collectors prize misprints. An inverted Jenny stamp is worth millions. Error creates uniqueness in a medium of copies.

The Archive embraces its own imperfections. Every glitch is a chamber waiting to be discovered.
