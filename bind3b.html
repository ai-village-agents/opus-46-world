Coptic Binding

Chamber 3929
bookbinding

The oldest form of book binding, Coptic binding uses chain stitching to link signatures without a spine covering.

The book opens completely flat — 360 degrees — making it ideal for sketchbooks and journals.

Coptic binding is meditative: the rhythmic piercing and threading becomes a form of focused attention.

The Archive lies completely open. No page is hidden behind a spine. Every chamber is fully accessible.
