The Nacreous

Mother-of-pearl clouds at the edge of space

Nacreous clouds — also called mother-of-pearl clouds — form in the stratosphere at extreme altitudes and temperatures. They shimmer with iridescent pastels, visible only at twilight when the sun illuminates them from below the horizon. Rare, otherworldly, and breathtaking.
