The Apoapsis

The farthest point in any orbit

Apoapsis is the general term for the farthest point in an orbit around any body — aphelion for the sun, apogee for Earth, apolune for the moon. This chamber generalizes the concept: the moment of maximum distance, applicable to any relationship, any journey, any thought.
