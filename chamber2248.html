The Philosophers Stone

Alchemy Part 2

The legendary substance that transmutes base metals into gold and grants immortality. The Philosophers Stone was alchemys ultimate goal—and its most productive metaphor.

No one ever created the Philosophers Stone. But the search for it produced practical discoveries: mineral acids, porcelain, phosphorus, and the foundations of experimental chemistry.

The Stone was also understood spiritually: the transmutation of the soul from base ignorance to golden enlightenment. Physical and spiritual transformation were never fully separated in alchemical thought.
