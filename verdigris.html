The Verdigris

Watch copper thoughts turn green with age

In this chamber, every thought arrives bright as new copper — gleaming, sharp-edged, catching light. But time works its quiet alchemy here. Ideas oxidize. Certainties develop a green patina.

The verdigris is not decay. It is transformation. It is the proof that something was once exposed to the air of the world, that it weathered storms and morning dew alike.

Touch the copper plates along the walls. Watch your fingerprints slowly bloom green where certainty meets doubt.
