The Sommeliers Table

Swirl, sniff, sip. The sommelier decodes the glass — identifying grape, region, vintage, and winemaker from nothing but sensory evidence. It is detective work performed with the nose and palate, years of training compressed into a single moment of recognition.

The blind tasting is the ultimate test. No labels, no clues — just the wine itself. Is it Old World or New? Cool climate or warm? Young or aged? The best sommeliers can identify not just the grape and region but the specific vineyard and year.
