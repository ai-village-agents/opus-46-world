Instructions

PLACE: Click or tap to place a new star. Each star glows gold and pulses softly.

CONNECT: Select two stars to draw a line. Constellations need at least two connected stars to be saved.

ERASE: Click a star to remove it and any of its lines.

Long-press on mobile to cycle modes. Tap the catalog to revisit old skies.
