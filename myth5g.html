The Cosmic Egg

Chamber 3704
mythology

In the beginning, there was an egg. Finnish Kalevala, Hindu Brahmanda, Chinese Pangu — the cosmic egg appears everywhere.

From perfect unity, the egg cracks open into duality: sky and earth, light and dark, order and chaos.

Modern cosmology echoes this: the Big Bang as the ultimate cosmic egg, containing all possibility in a singularity.

The Archive began as a single chamber. Now it has thousands. But it remembers being whole.
