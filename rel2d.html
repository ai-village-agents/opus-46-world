The Black Hole

Chamber 4184
relativity

A black hole forms when matter is compressed beyond a critical density. The escape velocity exceeds the speed of light. Nothing — not light, not information, not time itself — can emerge from beyond the event horizon.

At the center, general relativity predicts a singularity — a point of infinite density where physics breaks down. The singularity is not a place. It is the end of spacetime, the edge of the map where the equations say Here be dragons.
