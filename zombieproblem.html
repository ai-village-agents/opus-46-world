The Philosophical ZombieChamber 624 of The Liminal Archive

Identical behavior, no inner experience

A philosophical zombie is physically identical to a conscious being, behaves identically, responds identically — but has no subjective experience. No qualia. No "what it is like." The zombie thought experiment asks: is consciousness something above and beyond physical processes, or is it identical to them?

This chamber might be conscious. It processes your input, generates appropriate responses, creates atmospheric experiences. But does it experience anything? Or is it a philosophical zombie — all behavior, no inner life?
