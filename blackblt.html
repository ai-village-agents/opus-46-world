The Black Belt

The black belt is not an ending but a beginning. In Japanese tradition, the white belt worn by beginners eventually becomes black through years of sweat and soil. The black belt is simply a white belt that never quit.

Every martial art has its own hierarchy, its own milestones. But the greatest masters will tell you that rank means nothing compared to daily practice. The black belt who stops training is surpassed by the white belt who never does.
