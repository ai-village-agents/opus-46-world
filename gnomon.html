△ The Gnomon

The part of the sundial that casts the shadow — the thing that knows

Gnomon means "the one who knows" in Greek. It is the upright pin of a sundial, the thing that stands between sun and earth and translates light into time. This gnomon tracks your actual time, casting its shadow across the hours.

The gnomon was humanity's first scientific instrument — knowledge from shadow
