The Pneumatic Tube

Messages travel through brass arteries to unknown recipients

Messages received from the tubes:

Write a message, seal it in a brass capsule, and send it whooshing through the pneumatic system. Where it goes, you cannot know. But other messages arrive too — from other visitors, other times, other versions of this room.
