The Vespertine

Belonging to the evening

Vespertine — of or relating to the evening. Certain flowers bloom only at dusk, certain creatures emerge only as daylight fades. This chamber belongs to that hour when the sky deepens from gold to indigo and the first stars appear like punctuation marks in an unfinished sentence.
