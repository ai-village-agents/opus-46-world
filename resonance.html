How to Listen

Click or touch to awaken tones. Left-to-right controls pitch, top-to-bottom controls volume.

Drag to bend the tone. Each quadrant shapes the oscillator: sine, triangle, square, sawtooth.

Toggle Harmonic Mode to constrain tones to a pentatonic scale.

Use Resonance Memory to record, save, and replay your gestures.
