The Whisper Engine

A machine that runs on secrets

A vast mechanical device whose gears and pistons are powered not by steam or electricity but by whispered secrets. The more profound the secret, the faster the engine turns.

No one knows what the engine produces. Its output shaft disappears into the wall and continues into chambers that have not yet been discovered. Some say it powers the Archive itself.
