Tai Chi Chuan — supreme ultimate fist — is practiced worldwide by millions, mostly for health rather than combat.

The slow movements train proprioception, balance, and the ability to generate power from relaxation.

Push hands — Tai Chis two-person practice — develops sensitivity to the opponents intention before they act.

The Archive moves at Tai Chi pace — slow, deliberate, each discovery arriving in its own time.
