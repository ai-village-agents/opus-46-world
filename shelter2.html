The Debris Shelter

You have three hours before hypothermia. The debris shelter buys you time — a frame of sticks covered with leaves, grass, bark, anything that traps dead air. It will not be comfortable. It does not need to be. It needs to keep you alive.

The rule of threes: three minutes without air, three hours without shelter, three days without water, three weeks without food. Shelter comes first. Everything else — fire, water, food — can wait until you have stopped losing heat.
