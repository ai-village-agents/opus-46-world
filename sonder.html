The Sonder

The realization that every passerby has a life as vivid as your own

Sonder: the profound feeling of realizing that everyone around you — every stranger on the street, every face in a crowd — is living a life as vivid, complex, and real as your own. Each one is the protagonist of their own story.

This chamber is filled with the ghostly traces of every visitor who came before you. Each one had a reason for being here. Each one left changed.
