Fire Ecology

Chamber 3982
forestry

Many forest ecosystems require fire. Sequoias cannot reproduce without it. Longleaf pine depends on regular burns.

A century of fire suppression in American forests created fuel loads that now produce catastrophic megafires.

Prescribed burns — deliberate, controlled fires — are being reintroduced, often following indigenous burning practices.

The Archive may need its own prescribed burns — periodic clearing that makes space for new growth.
