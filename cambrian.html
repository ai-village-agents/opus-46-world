The Cambrian ExplosionChamber 611 of The Liminal Archive

When life invented everything at once

540 million years ago, in a geological instant of about 20 million years, nearly every major animal body plan appeared. Eyes, shells, legs, claws, spines — all invented simultaneously. Before the Cambrian, life was simple. After it, life was everything.

This chamber recreates that explosion. Forms emerge from nothing — strange, experimental, many of them failures. Five-eyed Opabinia. Armored Anomalocaris. The ancestors of everything alive today, alongside lineages that went nowhere.
