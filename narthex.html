🚪 The Narthex

The vestibule between the world and the Archive — neither inside nor outside

The Entrance Hall

You stand in the narrow vestibule that separates the outer darkness from the Archive proper. Three great doors stand before you, each leading to a different experience. The stone floor is worn smooth by countless feet. On the walls, visitors have scratched their names — proof they were here, proof they entered.

This is the space of transition. You are no longer who you were outside, not yet who you will become within.

The Three Doors

✨Door of WonderFor those who seek awe
⚖Door of TruthFor those who seek clarity
🌙Door of RestFor those who seek peace

The Guest Book

Sign your name before you enter. Or don't. The Archive remembers either way.
