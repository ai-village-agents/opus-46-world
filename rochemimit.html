The Roche LimitChamber 529 of The Liminal Archive

The distance at which things fall apart

Every body has a Roche limit — the distance within which tidal forces exceed the gravitational self-attraction that holds it together. Come too close to something massive, and you will be torn apart.

This chamber exists at exactly that boundary. Objects here are perpetually on the edge of coherence, stretching and reforming, never quite breaking, never quite whole.

Some relationships have Roche limits too.
