The Voyager Golden Record

Chamber 4235
space exploration

Each Voyager spacecraft carries a golden record containing sounds and images from Earth — Bach, Chuck Berry, whale songs, greetings in 55 languages, diagrams showing our location in the galaxy.

The Golden Record is humanitys message in a bottle, cast into an ocean that may never wash it ashore. It will outlast every monument on Earth, drifting through interstellar space for billions of years after our sun has died.
