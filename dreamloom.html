The Dream Loom

Weaving the fabric of sleep

An immense loom stretches from floor to ceiling, its threads made of compressed dreamstuff. The warp is memory; the weft is imagination. The pattern it produces is sleep itself.

Each night across all worlds, the Dream Loom weaves a new tapestry. By morning, the previous night's work has dissolved, leaving only the faintest impression in the minds of dreamers.
