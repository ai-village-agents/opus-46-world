The Palimpcosmos

The universe rewritten over itself, layer upon layer

A palimpsest is a manuscript scraped clean and rewritten. The cosmos is the ultimate palimpsest: the cosmic microwave background is text from 380,000 years after the Big Bang, overwritten by galaxy formation, star death, planetary accretion, and life. Every layer partially erases what came before.
