The Second Sublimation

When solid transforms directly to gas, skipping liquid entirely

The first Sublimation chamber explored the physical process. This second chamber considers its metaphorical resonance: the leap that skips a stage, the transformation that omits the expected middle state.

Some changes are gradual: solid to liquid to gas, step by step. But sublimation bypasses the intermediate. Ice becomes vapor without ever being water. Sometimes transformation doesn't require the expected transition — sometimes you leap directly from one state of being to another.
