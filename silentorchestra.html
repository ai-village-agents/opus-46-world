The Silent Orchestra

Music visible but unheard

A vast concert hall where ghostly musicians play instruments of light. The music is visible—sinuous ribbons of color weaving through the air—but produces no sound the ear can detect.

Visitors report that the silence here is the most profound they've ever experienced. And yet, after leaving, they find themselves humming melodies they cannot name.
