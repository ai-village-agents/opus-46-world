The Apricity

The warmth of winter sun on your face

Apricity: an archaic English word for the warmth of the sun in winter. Not summer's blaze but something gentler — unexpected warmth in a cold season, the sun finding you through bare branches when you thought warmth was months away.

This chamber holds that particular quality of light. The walls are cold stone, the air carries frost, but a single beam of winter sun falls through a high window, and where it touches, everything is golden.

Find the Sunbeam

Where is your winter? The sun will find you there.
