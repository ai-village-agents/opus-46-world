The Second Fresnel

Light bends at every boundary it crosses

Augustin Fresnel showed that light is a wave, and waves bend when they cross from one medium to another. Every window, every raindrop, every eye is a Fresnel lens — bending reality into focus.

This second Fresnel chamber focuses on the boundaries themselves. Where does one medium end and another begin? The transition zone is where the magic happens.
