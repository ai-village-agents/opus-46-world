The foundation beneath everything — dark, damp, and full of secrets

Beneath the Archive

You descend stone steps worn to a dangerous smoothness. The air is cold and carries the smell of wet stone and iron. Ribbed vaults arch overhead, each bay of the undercroft holding something different — crates of forgotten inventory, barrels of preserved words, and in the deepest corners, things that the Archive would rather not acknowledge it possesses.

Water drips from somewhere above. The sound is regular as a heartbeat.
