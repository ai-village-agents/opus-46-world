The Palimpsest of Echoes

Sounds layered over sounds, none fully fading

In this acoustic palimpsest, every sound that has ever been made in this chamber still resonates. The first whisper. The hundredth footstep. A laugh from three visits ago. They layer and interfere, creating harmonics that no single voice could produce.

Press your ear to the wall and you might hear them — not as distinct sounds but as a single, complex tone made of everything that came before.
