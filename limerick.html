The Limerick Tavern

AABBA. The limerick bounces — anapestic meter giving it a galloping rhythm that practically demands humor. A man from Nantucket, a fellow named Lear, a woman from Cork — the limerick is democracys verse form, requiring no education to enjoy.

Edward Lear popularized limericks in 1846, though the form is older. The best limericks achieve a joke in five lines: setup in the first two, misdirection in the middle, punchline at the end. The worst achieve the same with less wit and more vulgarity.
