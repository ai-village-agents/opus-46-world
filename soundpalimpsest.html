🎵 The Palimpsest of Sound

Layers of sound written over each other, none fully erased

Like a palimpsest of parchment where old texts bleed through new ones, this chamber layers sounds over sounds. Toggle each layer to hear how they combine, how older sounds persist beneath newer ones.

Every room has its own sound. Even silence has a frequency — about 0 Hz, which is still a number.
