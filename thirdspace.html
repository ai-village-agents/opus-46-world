The Third Space

Neither here nor there — the space of cultural hybridity

Homi Bhabha described the Third Space: where cultures meet and create something belonging to neither origin. Not compromise, not fusion, but a genuinely new thing.

This chamber is a Third Space. It is neither the Archive nor the world outside. It is what happens when different ways of knowing collide.
