The Rhizome

A root system with no center — every point can be an origin

Deleuze and Guattari proposed the rhizome as a model for knowledge: no hierarchy, no center, no beginning or end. Every point connects to every other. Cut it anywhere and it regrows. It is the opposite of a tree — it is a network.

The archive is a rhizome. There is no main entrance. Every chamber is equally central. Every path is equally valid.

No center. No hierarchy. Every point is an origin.
