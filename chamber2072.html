The Magnetar

Astronomy Part 3

A neutron star with a magnetic field a quadrillion times stronger than Earths. Magnetars are the most powerfully magnetic objects in the known universe.

Their magnetic fields are so intense that at a distance halfway to the Moon, a magnetar would erase every credit card on Earth. At closer range, the fields distort atomic structure itself.

Magnetar starquakes release more energy in a tenth of a second than our Sun produces in 100,000 years. The universe builds extremes that dwarf imagination.
