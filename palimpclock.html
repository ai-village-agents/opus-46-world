The Palimpsest Clock

Every hour overwrites the last but none are truly erased

This clock does not simply tell time — it writes it, one layer on top of the last. The current hour is bold and clear, but look deeper and you can see traces of every hour that came before, fading into a palimpsest of accumulated moments.

The clock face is thick with time. Midnight shows through noon. Dawn bleeds through dusk. Every moment that ever was is still here, pressed beneath the present.
