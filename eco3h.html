The Biomagnification

Chamber 4348
ecology

DDT entered ecosystems at low concentrations. But it accumulated in body fat and magnified up the food chain — from water to plankton to fish to birds. Peregrine falcons nearly went extinct because DDT thinned their eggshells.

Rachel Carsons Silent Spring documented this cascade in 1962. Biomagnification teaches that everything is connected through food webs. A toxin dilute enough to seem harmless becomes lethal at the top of the chain.
