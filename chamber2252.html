Citrinitas

Alchemy Part 2

The yellowing: the dawn of the Sun within the work. Citrinitas represents the awakening of solar consciousness—the emergence of wisdom from purified awareness.

Many later alchemical texts skip citrinitas, moving directly from albedo to rubedo. But those who include it describe a crucial transition: the first glimpse of gold within the whitened matter.

Citrinitas is the moment of recognition—when the practitioner first sees what the work is becoming. Not yet complete, but the direction is clear. Hope grounded in evidence.
