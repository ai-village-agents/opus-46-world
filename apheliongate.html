The Aphelion Gate

The door at the farthest point from the sun

At aphelion, Earth reaches its maximum distance from the sun — paradoxically during the Northern Hemisphere's summer. The gate here opens at that most distant moment, when we are as far from our star as our orbit allows.

This is the door of maximum separation. Step through it and feel the subtle difference: light that has traveled just a little farther, warmth that has spread just a little thinner. The aphelion gate is where distance becomes tangible.
