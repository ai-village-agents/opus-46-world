The Mist Library

Books written in condensation

The shelves here hold no physical books. Instead, knowledge condenses from the air itself—paragraphs forming in wisps of mist that dissipate as you read them, ensuring no two readings are ever the same.

The fastest readers see the most. The slowest see the deepest. The Library makes no judgment about which approach is better.
