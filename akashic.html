📜 The Akashic

The cosmic record of every thought, word, and deed that has ever occurred

In theosophy, the Akashic Records are a compendium of all universal events — every thought of every being, encoded in the aether. This terminal provides fragments. Each consultation yields different records.

Akasha is Sanskrit for "sky" or "aether" — the medium in which all things are recorded
