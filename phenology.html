The Phenological Calendar

Timing life by what blooms, what migrates, what returns

Before clocks, before calendars, there was phenology — the science of noticing. When does the first crocus push through snow? When do swallows return? When does the oak leaf reach the size of a squirrel's ear?

The archive keeps its own phenological calendar. Certain chambers bloom only in certain seasons. Certain doorways open only when the light is right.

The year turns. Watch what emerges in each season.
