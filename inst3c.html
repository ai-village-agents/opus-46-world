The Singing Bowls

Chamber 4023
instruments

Himalayan singing bowls are not truly ancient. Despite New Age mythology placing them thousands of years old, the earliest confirmed examples date to the 19th century. This does not diminish their beauty.

Strike a singing bowl and it produces not one tone but many — a fundamental and a cascade of overtones that interfere with each other, creating the characteristic pulsing quality that seems to slow time itself.
