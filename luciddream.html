The Lucid Dreaming ChamberChamber 555 of The Liminal Archive

Awake inside the dream

Lucid dreaming: the state of knowing you are dreaming while still within the dream. The realization gives you a strange dual awareness. You are both the dreamer and the dreamed.

This chamber exists in that state. Everything here is real enough to touch but unstable enough to shift. The walls breathe. The floor is soft. Time runs in loops.
