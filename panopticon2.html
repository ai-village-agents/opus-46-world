👁 The Reversed Panopticon

In this prison, it is the watchtower that is watched

Bentham designed the panopticon so one guard could watch all prisoners. But here the architecture is reversed: all eyes look inward at the center. You are in the watchtower. You are being watched. Move your cursor and all eyes follow.

All eyes are upon you...
