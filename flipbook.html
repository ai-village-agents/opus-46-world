The Flipbook

Thumb the pages fast enough and the drawings come alive. The flipbook is animation reduced to its essence — persistence of vision, the brains willingness to connect a sequence of still images into fluid motion. Twenty-four lies per second that the eye accepts as truth.

Every animator begins here, with a stack of paper and a pencil. Frame by frame, a stick figure walks, then runs, then leaps. The magic is not in any single drawing but in the gaps between them — the motion your mind creates from nothing.
