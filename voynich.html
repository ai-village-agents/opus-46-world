The Voynich ManuscriptChamber 597 of The Liminal Archive

A book no one can read

The Voynich manuscript: a 15th-century codex written in an unknown script, illustrated with unidentifiable plants, astronomical diagrams, and naked figures in green pools. Carbon-dated, authenticated, analyzed by the world's best cryptographers — and still completely unreadable.

Pages from the manuscript line this chamber's walls. The script flows in elegant curves that look like they should mean something. The plants are almost recognizable. The diagrams nearly make sense. Everything here is almost comprehensible, and that almost is maddening.
