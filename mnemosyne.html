The Mnemosyne

Mother of the Muses — Memory herself

Mnemosyne was a Titaness, the goddess of memory and mother of the nine Muses. The Greeks understood: without memory, there is no art. Every poem, every song, every story begins with remembering.

This chamber is Mnemosyne's domain. Here, memory is not storage but creation. Each act of remembering is an act of imagination. Each recollection is a new work of art.
