The Blackbody RadiationChamber 630 of The Liminal Archive

The perfect emitter at every temperature

A blackbody absorbs all radiation that hits it and re-emits it as thermal radiation. The spectrum depends only on temperature: cool bodies glow infrared, warm ones glow red, hot ones white, extremely hot ones blue. The blackbody spectrum was the puzzle that led Planck to invent quantum mechanics.

This chamber cycles through temperatures. Watch as the color shifts from invisible warmth to deep red to blinding white. Each color corresponds to a precise temperature. The universe speaks in wavelengths.
