Ichigo Ichie

Tea Ceremony

One time, one meeting. Every tea ceremony is unique and unrepeatable. This gathering, these people, this moment will never occur again. Treasure it accordingly.

Ichigo ichie is not mere sentimentality—it is a practice of radical attention. If this meeting will never recur, then every detail deserves your full presence.

The impermanence that makes each ceremony precious also makes it poignant. The beauty of the tea room, the taste of the matcha, the presence of the guests—all temporary. All the more beautiful for it.
