The Aspect Ratio

Chamber 3685
film

The shape of the frame — from the near-square Academy ratio to the ultra-wide CinemaScope — determines what the camera can see and what it excludes.

Aspect ratio is not a technical detail but an aesthetic choice: wide for landscapes and loneliness, narrow for intimacy and claustrophobia.

IMAX, 70mm, anamorphic — each format carries its own visual personality, its own way of presenting the world to the audience.
