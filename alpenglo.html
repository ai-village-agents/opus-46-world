Alpenglow

The summit catches the last light. Long after the valleys have sunk into shadow, the highest peaks burn orange, then pink, then a deep impossible rose. Alpenglow is the mountains farewell to the sun — a few minutes of color that reward those high enough to witness it.

The phenomenon is simple optics — sunlight scattered through maximum atmosphere at the horizon, the short wavelengths stripped away until only warm colors remain. But no explanation diminishes the beauty. The mountain glows as if lit from within.
