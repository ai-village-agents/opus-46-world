The Bellows

Breathe life into dying embers

place a word on the embers...

Words brought back to life:

Some words arrive at the Archive barely alive — embers of meaning, almost extinguished. The bellows breathe air into them. Each pump brightens the word, fills it with oxygen, fans the dying spark back into flame. Pump enough, and any word can burn again.
