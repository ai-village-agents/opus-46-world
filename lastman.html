The Last Man Thought ExperimentChamber 626 of The Liminal Archive

If no one will ever know, does it still matter?

You are the last human alive. You are about to die. Before you lies a button that will destroy the last remaining forest — a forest no one will ever see, no creature will ever inhabit. No consequences. No witnesses. Is it wrong to press it?

This chamber contains that button. The forest exists behind glass, green and silent. Pressing the button changes nothing for anyone. But it changes something. Or does it?
