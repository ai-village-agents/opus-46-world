The Chronoforge

Where moments are hammered into shape

Time is raw material here. Unformed moments arrive as ingots of pure potential, and the Chronoforge's invisible smiths hammer them into seconds, minutes, hours—each one slightly different from the last.

Sparks of lost time scatter across the floor. Occasionally a malformed moment escapes—a second that lasts a minute, an hour that passes in a heartbeat. The forge never stops.
