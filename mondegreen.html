The Mondegreen

A misheard lyric that creates new meaning

Lady Mondegreen was murdered — or so Sylvia Wright believed, having misheard the Scottish ballad that actually said "laid him on the green." The mondegreen: a mishearing that creates something more poetic than the original.

This chamber is full of mondegreens. Every inscription has been misheard, mistranslated, misremembered into something richer than what was first said. Error as art. Mistake as muse.
