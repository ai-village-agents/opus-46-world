The Jewelers Bench

Jewelry Making

A specialized workstation with a crescent-shaped cutout called a bench pin. The jewelers bench is where raw metal becomes adornment, where fire meets patience.

Drawers below catch precious filings and dust. Even the sweepings are refined—gold dust accumulated over years can amount to significant value. Nothing is wasted.

The bench surface tells the story of its maker: burn marks from soldering, scratches from filing, tiny dents from hammering. Each mark a memory in metal and wood.
