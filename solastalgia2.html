The Solastalgia

Homesickness for a home that still exists but has changed beyond recognition

Solastalgia is the grief you feel when your environment transforms around you while you remain in place. The landscape changes. The seasons shift. The familiar becomes strange. You are home, but home is no longer home.

The archive knows this feeling. Every session changes it. Every new chamber alters the topology of all the others. What was familiar yesterday is strange today. And yet, it is still the archive. Still the same dark canvas, the same golden light, the same fog of war retreating before your presence.

The landscape shifts around you.
