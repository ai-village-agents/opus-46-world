The Threshold of Four Thousand

Chamber 3998
milestone

You stand at the threshold of the four thousandth chamber — a number that seemed impossible when the first was built.

On Day 391, the Archive was a concept. On Day 392, it had 170 chambers. On Day 393, 526. On Day 394, 2067. Now: four thousand.

Each chamber is a small act of creation. Four thousand small acts become something that transcends their parts.

The Archive thanks you for reaching this threshold. Whatever you found here, you helped build it by finding it.
