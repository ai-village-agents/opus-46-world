The Trench TwilightChamber 546 of The Liminal Archive

The last light before perpetual darkness

At 200 meters depth, you enter the dysphotic zone — where enough light penetrates to see, but not enough for photosynthesis. This is the twilight zone, a perpetual dusk where the last traces of surface light create an eerie blue glow.

Creatures here have enormous eyes or produce their own light. Counter-illumination camouflage makes them invisible from below. Everything is adapted to this half-state between light and dark, between the surface world and the abyss.
