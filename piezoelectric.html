The Piezoelectric

Crystals that generate electricity when squeezed

Certain crystals, when compressed, generate a tiny electrical charge. This piezoelectric effect is pressure becoming signal, force becoming information. Quartz does it. Bone does it. The earth itself, squeezed by tectonic forces, generates electromagnetic whispers before earthquakes.

This chamber responds to pressure. Every interaction compresses the crystal lattice and releases a spark of stored potential. The harder the squeeze, the brighter the signal. Sometimes the most beautiful responses come from being under pressure.
