⊞ The Enfilade

A suite of rooms with doors aligned — an infinite vista through doorways

In Baroque architecture, an enfilade is a sequence of rooms whose doors are all in alignment, creating a vista that stretches to a vanishing point. Stand at one end and see through every room at once.
