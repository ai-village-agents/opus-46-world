The Antipodal Point

The exact opposite point on Earth from where you stand

Draw a line straight down through the center of the Earth and out the other side. Where it emerges is your antipodal point — the place on the planet farthest from you, the spot that is as far away as geography allows while remaining on the surface.

Most antipodal points are in the ocean, because most of the Earth is ocean. The land at your feet likely has water at its antipode. You stand on solid ground; your opposite self treads water. This chamber is the room on the other side of everything.
